The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications built along the historical northern borders of China to protect the Chinese states and empires. The most well-known sections were built by the Ming dynasty (1368–1644). It is a magnificent structure spanning thousands of miles, showcasing incredible ancient engineering and strategic defense.
